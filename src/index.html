How musically similar top songs are?

Our charts are getting sadder, but almost equally danceable.

By Ruobing Su

When we talk about pop music these days, many of them sound very similar - from how the lyrics are written, to who is the actual or featuring singer. The biggest hits since 2010 up to 2018 can be Drake, Ed Sheeran, Calvin Harris, Rihanna, David Guetta, Foster The People, and Jay Z. Many of the names have been constantly on the hot 100 chart over the years, and sometimes it is too easy to predict a pop singer is going to make it to Hot 100 simply based on his/her fame and music genre.

Is there something unique about today’s hits—artists taking fewer risks and creating a narrower range of sounds? Using the data set extracted from Spotify’s web API, I exam how pop music diverse or change over the year in terms of audio features by visualizing the hot tracks of each year’s hot 100 tracks.
